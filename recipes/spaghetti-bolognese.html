Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with ground beef and a rich tomato sauce. This hearty meal is perfect for weeknights and dinner parties alike.

Easy to make and deliciously satisfying, it's a favorite for kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 250g ground beef
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes and oregano. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve sauce over spaghetti and top with Parmesan if desired.
