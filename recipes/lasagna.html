Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of wide, flat pasta sheets (also called lasagna) alternating with various fillings, typically baked in the oven until bubbly and golden brown

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese Filling: Ricotta and Parmesan
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook meat with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce and simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mix with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna reached a golden brown color.
